NEXOURA Decision · 001

Pick brand direction for NEXOURA Studio public homepage

Context: Studio launches publicly in 3 weeks and the homepage is the first impression for prospective B2B clients. We have three drafts in review and must lock direction this week to leave time for copy + assets.

Minimal

Black-and-white wordmark on near-empty hero. Pure typography, no gradient, single CTA.

  • Fastest to ship
  • Conveys focus
  • Easy A/B test baseline
  • Indistinguishable from 50 competitors
  • No brand recall

Bold

Full NEXOURA gradient hero, animated ring motif, large kinetic headline. High-energy.

  • Maximum brand recall
  • Stands out in screenshots
  • Reuses ring asset
  • Reads "consumer" not "enterprise"
  • Slower mobile LCP

Premium

Restrained dark canvas with one gradient accent on headline + CTA. Generous whitespace, fine-grain typography, subtle ring in corner.

  • Reads enterprise / serious
  • Brand visible but not loud
  • Print-friendly screenshots
  • Slightly more design polish needed
  • Higher copy-quality bar
Recommendation: Premium — our B2B audience expects restraint and polish; Bold reads consumer, Minimal kills brand recall in screenshots.
