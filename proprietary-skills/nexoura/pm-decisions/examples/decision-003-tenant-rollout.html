NEXOURA Decision · 003

Pick Phase 6 Tenant Zero rollout strategy

Context: Tenant Zero infrastructure is feature-complete and we need to pick how aggressively to onboard the first real client cohort. Choice locks the SLA, support model, and engineering bandwidth for Q3.

Soft launch

Invite 3 friendly tenants, manual onboarding, no SLA promised. Iterate fast in private.

  • Lowest support load
  • Room to break things
  • Faster product-feedback loop
  • No revenue signal yet
  • Delays public proof

Cohort launch

Open a 10-tenant cohort with a published SLA. Public announcement, full onboarding flow live.

  • Real revenue signal
  • Forces SLA discipline
  • Public proof for sales
  • Higher support burden
  • SLA breach risk on day 1

Open beta

Self-serve sign-up, no cohort cap, "beta" disclaimer. Maximum surface area.

  • Widest learning surface
  • No gatekeeping cost
  • Support load unbounded
  • Quality bar unenforceable
  • Brand risk if it wobbles
Recommendation: Soft launch — we have not yet hit the stability bar for a published SLA; 3 friendly tenants gives us real signal without trading away the brand on day 1.
